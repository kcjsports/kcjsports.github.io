## Hatchling Robot: January 2024 - May 2024

| ![Claw](/images/MISC/Claw.png)  | ![Full Robot](/images/MISC/Hbot.png)  |
| :-----------------------------: | :-----------------------------------: |
|        *Claw*                   |           *Full Robot*                |

**Goal**:
- Pick up 1.5in diameter foam balls

**Specs**:
- Three finger claw end-effector controlled by the rotation of a micro servo
- Custom lazy Susan bearing allows servo rotation of crane system
- Hoist transmission has unused clutch mechanism for future additions
- Crankshaft mechanism used to raise and lower catcher
- Electronics: 2 SG90 micro servos, Arduino Uno R3, 3 DC motors, 2 L298n motor drivers, HC-05 Bluetooth module
